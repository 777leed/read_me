Rosemary District

It was a dark night as usual, the broken moon shinning its dim light as if it was trying so hard to guide him through these dead-silent hallways, people were dying in the corners of every street without much of a noise.no one would care even if they screamed their lungs out. It's a very selfish world and there is no one to blame for that.
